By using the PAH risk score calculator the user agrees and consents to be bound to the terms of use, as follows:

  • The risk score calculator variables and cut-offs are based on the REVEAL registry, REVEAL 2.0 and REVEAL Lite 2. [1-3] The sum of the points in addition to the correction factor of 6 can be classified into 3 strata (low-risk, intermediate-risk and high-risk), corresponding to estimated 1-year mortality rates < 5%, 5 - 20 %, respectively > 20 %, according to the 2022 ESC/ERS guidelines.[1-6]
  • To generate a score using REVEAL 2.0, 7 parameters out of 13 are required. Age and sex are counted as 1 parameter.[2,6]
  • To generate a score using REVEAL Lite 2, at least 3 variables out of 6 are required, where 2 out of the 3 most predictive must be included (NYHA/WHO-FC, BNP/NTproBNP, and 6MWD).[3,6]
  • The risk score calculator was adopted from the REVEAL 2.0 and REVEAL Lite 2, developed by Raymond Benza et al.[1-3,6]
  • The user takes full responsibility, for all data input, calculated risk scores, and decision making.
  • The developers are not liable for any errors or inaccuracies in the algorithms or content within or related to the calculation tool.
  • The final decision making has to be made by the user on a comprehensive and balanced clinical judgement in relation to the clinical presentation. The risk score calculator serves only as a supporting tool.
  • The risk score calculator may be updated in accordance with growing body of evidence.
